About Our Firm

Handyman is an online platform for skills on demand around the craft trades (plumbing, electricity, refrigeration, masonry, air conditioning etc ...). It aims to simplify the search for a good craftsman for homework. Thanks to its various partners, Handyman offers quality services with certified equipment by allowing these customers to choose their service in a few clicks and schedule it according to their availability.

More than a simple connection, Handyman intends to make life easier for these customers by connecting them to the best craftsmen at all times and in all places, and respecting three promises: a simple, quality experience with the most commitment.
